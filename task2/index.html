Battleground Mobile India

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI),[5] previously known as PUBG Mobile India,[6] is a version of PUBG Mobile, available in India.[7][8][9][10] It is an online multiplayer battle royale game by Krafton.[11] The game was initially released on 2 July 2021 for Android devices,[11] and on 18 August 2021 for iOS devices.
